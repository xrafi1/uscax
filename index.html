A Brief About USCA

The United Student Council of Assam (USCA) is the leading student-led body devoted to the empowerment, representation, and cultural preservation of Manipuri-speaking students in Assam. As a dedicated and non-political platform, USCA bridges the gap between students and leadership, nurturing unity and a collective voice for a stronger future.

Uniting Manipuri Students

USCA acts as a united front for all Manipuri students in Assam, creating a strong network to encourage mutual support, cultural pride, and educational growth.

Empowering Youth Leadership

USCA is dedicated to nurturing leadership and confidence among Manipuri youth—providing platforms and resources for their educational, social, and personal development.

Preserving Identity & Rights

USCA stands firm in advocating for the cultural identity and rights of the Manipuri community through campaigns, awareness programs, and student engagement.

Driven by Purpose, Not Politics

USCA is a completely non-political organization, focusing only on the upliftment of Manipuri students. Our mission is clear: serve and support the community—without bias.

Sustaining Manipuri Culture

With a strong commitment to heritage, USCA promotes traditional practices, language, and festivals, ensuring Manipuri culture thrives for generations to come.
